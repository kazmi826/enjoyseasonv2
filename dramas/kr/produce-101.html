❓ FAQ about Produce 101

What is Produce 101 about?
Produce 101 (프로듀스 101) is a reality music group survival show on Mnet. It is a large-scale project in which the public "produces" a unit group by choosing members from a pool of 101 trainees from 46 entertainment companies as well as the group's concept, debut song, and group name.
How many episodes does Produce 101 have?
Produce 101 has a total of 22 episodes spread across 2 season(s). It first aired on 2016-01-22.
Who stars in Produce 101?
The main cast of Produce 101 includes Kwon Hyun-bin, Park Sung-woo, Lee You-jin, Kwon BoA, Shin Yoo-mi.
What is the rating of Produce 101?
Produce 101 has a rating of 6.7/10 based on 12 votes. It is a ended Reality series.
