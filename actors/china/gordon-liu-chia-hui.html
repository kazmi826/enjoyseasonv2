Gordon Liu Chia-Hui

China Actor, Actress

🌍 China 🎂 1951-08-22 📍 Foshan, China Age: 75 ⏳ Since 1951 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Gordon Liu Chia-Hui? Gordon Liu Chia-Hui (劉家輝) is a Chinese martial arts film actor and martial artist. He became famous for playing the lead role of San Te in The 36th Chamber of Shaolin and its sequels. He also played kung fu master Pai Mei in Kill Bill: Volume 2 (2004), and Johnny Mo in Kill Bill: Volume 1 (2003).
