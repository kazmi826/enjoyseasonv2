Who is Damian Lau Chung-Yan? Damian Lau Chung-Yan (Chinese: 劉松仁, born 14 October 1949) is a Hong Kong film and television actor, executive producer and film director. Lau has starred in many television drama series of various genres, produced by Hong Kong's TVB and ATV.
