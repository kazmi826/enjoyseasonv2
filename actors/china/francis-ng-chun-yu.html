Who is Francis Ng Chun-Yu? Francis Ng Chun-Yu (Chinese: 吳鎮宇, born 21 December 1961) is a Hong Kong actor and director. He is known for his roles in the TVB series Triumph in the Skies and in films such as Young and Dangerous, Once Upon a Time in Triad Society and The Mission.
