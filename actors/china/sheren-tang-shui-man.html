Sheren Tang Shui-Man

China Actor, Actress

🌍 China 🎂 1966-03-02 📍 Hong Kong, China Age: 60 ⏳ Since 1966 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Sheren Tang Shui-Man? Sheren Tang Shui-Man (Chinese: 鄧萃雯; born 2 March 1966) is a Hong Kong actress. She is best known for her roles in the mega-hit Hong Kong TVB drama series War and Beauty, as well as La Femme Desperado, The Family Link, Rosy Business and No Regrets. Tang is the first Hong Kong actress to win the Best Actress Award in two consecutive years.
