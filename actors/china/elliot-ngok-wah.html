Who is Elliot Ngok Wah? Elliot Ngok Wah, also known as Yueh Hua (Chinese: 岳華; 14 July 1942 – 20 October 2018), , was a Shanghai-born Hong Kong actor, later based in Canada, with Shaw Brothers Studio and TVB. One of the most versatile and prolific leading actors of Shaw Brothers Studio, he starred in five to ten films per year in his heyday, playing roles ranging from foolish drunks to scholarly warriors.
