Who is Tong Liya? Tong Liya is a Chinese actress. Her nickname is Ya Ya. She was born in Qapqal Xibe Autonomous County, Yili State, Xinjiang, and is of the Xibo ethnicity. She is known for portraying Tong Suyan on the Chinese TV series Gong. Wikipedia
