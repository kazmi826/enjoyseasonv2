He Hongshan

China Actor, Actress

🌍 China 🎂 1987-04-10 📍 Chongqing, China Age: 39 ⏳ Since 1987 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is He Hongshan? He Hong Shan is a Chinese actress, photographer, and model from Chongqing, China. She graduated from Chongqing University of Humanities and Technology (重庆人文科技学院), where she studied acting. She made her film debut in 2008 with "Street’s A-Qiang" (街上的阿强).
