Ekin Cheng Yee-Kin

British Hong Kong Actor, Actress

🌍 British Hong Kong 🎂 1967-10-04 📍 British Hong Kong Age: 59 ⏳ Since 1967 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Ekin Cheng Yee-Kin? Ekin Cheng Yee-Kin (Chinese: 鄭伊健, born 4 October 1967) is a Hong Kong actor and Cantopop singer. Early in his career he used the name Dior (because that was what it sounded like when his younger sister tried to call him ) as a first name. He has also been referred to as Noodle Cheng, after a popular noodle product with a similar name. Currently Ekin is the name used.
