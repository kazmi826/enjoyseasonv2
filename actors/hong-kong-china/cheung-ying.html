Who is Cheung Ying? Cheung Ying (Chinese: 张瑛) (January 25, 1919 – December 14, 1984) was a Cantonese actor from Hong Kong. Cheung appeared in the movie Roar of the People, which starred Walter Cho Tat-Wah. He also appeared in Back to the Motherland! (1937), which starred director and father of Fung Hak-on, Fung Bo Bo and Feng Feng.
