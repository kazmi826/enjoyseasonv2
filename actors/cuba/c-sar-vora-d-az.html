About César Évora Díaz César Évora Díaz (Havana, November 4, 1959) is a Cuban actor who has developed most of his career in Mexico. He studied Geophysics, to dedicate himself to the search for oil and minerals, until one day he decided to appear at a casting, where he was selected among 500 applicants. After working in his native country, he decided to settle permanently in Mexico. Lives in Mexico City. In 1999 he obtained Mexican nationality. children: Rafael, Mariana and Carla.
