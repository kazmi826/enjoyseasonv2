Alice Krige

South Africa Actor, Actress

🌍 South Africa 🎂 1954-06-28 📍 Upington, South Africa Age: 72 ⏳ Since 1954 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Alice Krige? Alice Maud Krige is a South African actress and producer. Her first feature film role was as the Gilbert and Sullivan singer Sybil Gordon in the 1981 Academy Award-winning film Chariots of Fire. Since then, she has played a variety of roles in a number of genres. Krige first played the role of the Borg Queen in the motion picture Star Trek: First Contact and reprised the role for the final episode of the television series Star Trek: Voyager. A year after the series ended, she reprised the role in "Borg Invasion 4-D" at Star Trek: The Experience.
