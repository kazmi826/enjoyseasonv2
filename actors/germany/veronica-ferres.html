Veronica Ferres

Germany Actor, Actress

🌍 Germany 🎂 1965-06-10 📍 Solingen, Germany Age: 61 ⏳ Since 1965 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Veronica Ferres? Veronica Maria Cäcilia Ferres (born 10 June 1965) is a German actress who gained fame as Pierre Richard's co-star in the French TV-movie Sans famille and as the horrible Mme. Thénardier in the 2000 French TV miniseries Les Misérables. She also starred in the Oscar-nominated German movie Schtonk (1992). In 2007 she played with Willem Dafoe and Jeff Goldblum in the international cinema production Adam Resurrected directed by Paul Schrader.
