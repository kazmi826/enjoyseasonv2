Mohamed Henedi

Egypt Actor, Actress

🌍 Egypt 🎂 1965-02-01 📍 Giza, Egypt Age: 61 ⏳ Since 1965 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Mohamed Henedi? Mohamed Henedy [Arabic: محمد هنيدى‎] is an Egyptian comedy actor born in Giza, Egypt, on 1 February 1965 and has gained a cinematic bachelor's degree. Henedi started his career in 1991 in short appearances in theaters and cinemas, and he achieved huge success in his two films Esma'eleya Rayeh Gaii and Sa'ede Fel Gam'a Al Amrekya. He later starred in the movies Hamam fi Amesterdam, Belya we Demagho el Alya, Saheb Sahbo and Andaleeb Al Dokki. Mohamed Henedi also dubbed the voices of Timon, Mike Wazowski and Homer Simpson for the Egyptian versions of The Lion King, Monsters, Inc., and The Simpsons respectively.
