Who is Zeeko Zaki? Zeeko Zaki (born January 18, 1990) is an American actor of Egyptian, Syrian and Turkish ancestry. He's best known for his roles as Agent Omar Adom 'OA' Zidan on CBS's series FBI (2018) and Akmal Barayev on the History Channel miniseries SIX. He was born in Alexandria, Egypt and immigrated to the United States when he was a one-month-old baby.
