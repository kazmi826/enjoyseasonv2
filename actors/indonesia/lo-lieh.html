Who is Lo Lieh? Wang Lap Tat (June 29, 1939 – November 2, 2002), better known by his stage name Lo Lieh, was an Indonesian-born Hong Kong actor, best known as Chao Chih-Hao in King Boxer (1972, aka Five Fingers of Death), Miyamoto in Fist of Fury II (1977) and General Tien Ta in The 36th Chamber of Shaolin (1978).
