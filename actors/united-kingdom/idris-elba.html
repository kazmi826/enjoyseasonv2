Idris Elba

British Actor, DJ, Producer

🌍 United Kingdom 🎂 September 6, 1972 📍 London, England Age: 54 ⏳ Since 1994–present 🎭 Crime, Action, Drama
Who is Idris Elba? Idris Elba (born September 6, 1972) is a British television, theatre, and film actor who has starred in both British and American productions. One of his first acting roles was in the soap opera Family Affairs. Since then he has worked in a variety of TV and movie projects including Ultraviolet, The Wire, No Good Deed and Zootopia.
