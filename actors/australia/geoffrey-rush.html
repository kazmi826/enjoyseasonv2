Geoffrey Rush

Australia Actor, Actress

🌍 Australia 🎂 1951-07-06 📍 Toowoomba, Queensland, Australia Age: 75 ⏳ Since 1951 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Geoffrey Rush? Geoffrey Roy Rush AC (born 6 July 1951) is an Australian actor. He is known for his eccentric leading man roles on stage and screen. He is among 24 people who have won the Triple Crown of Acting, having received an Academy Award, a Primetime Emmy Award and a Tony Award. He also received three British Academy Film Awards, two Golden Globe Awards, and four Screen Actors Guild Awards. Rush is the founding president of the Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts and was named the 2012 Australian of the Year.
