🌍 Australia 🎂 1969-08-21 📍 Gold Coast, Queensland, Australia Age: 57 ⏳ Since 1969 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Nathan Jones? Nathan Jones (born 21 August 1970) is an Australian actor, powerlifting champion, strongman and retired professional wrestler. He is best known for his time in World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He held the WWA World Heavyweight Championship once in World Wrestling All-Stars, and as an actor is known for his roles in films such as Troy (2004),Tom-Yum-Goong (2005), Fearless (2006),Conan the Barbarian (2011), Max Max: Fury Road (2015), Hobbs & Shaw (2019), and Mortal Kombat (2021), usually playing antagonists.
