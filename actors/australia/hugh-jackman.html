Hugh Jackman

Australian Actor, Singer

🌍 Australia 🎂 1968-10-12 📍 Sydney, Australia Age: 58 ⏳ Since 1994–present 🎭 Action, Musical, Drama
Who is Hugh Jackman? Hugh Michael Jackman (born 12 October 1968) is an Australian actor, singer, and producer. Beginning in theatre and television, Jackman landed his breakthrough role as Wolverine, playing it across the X-Men film franchise and in the Marvel Cinematic Universe from X-Men (2000) to Deadpool & Wolverine (2024). Prominent on both screen and stage, he has received various accolades, including a Primetime Emmy Award, a Grammy Award and two Tony Awards, along with nominations for an Academy Award and a British Academy Film Award. Jackman was appointed a Companion of the Order of Australia in 2019.
