Henry Golding

Malaysia Actor, Actress

🌍 Malaysia 🎂 1987-02-05 📍 Betong, Malaysia Age: 39 ⏳ Since 1987 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Henry Golding? Henry Ewan Golding (born 5 February 1987) is a Malaysian-British actor and television host. He has been a presenter on BBC's The Travel Show since 2014. He is known for his film work, playing the role of Nick Young in Crazy Rich Asians (2018), the title character in the action-adventure Snake Eyes (2021), Sean Townsend in the thriller A Simple Favor (2018), and Tom in the romantic comedy Last Christmas (2019), the latter two directed by Paul Feig.
