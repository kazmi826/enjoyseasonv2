Bassem Yakhour

Syrian Actor, Director

🌍 Syria 🎂 August 16, 1971 📍 Lattakia, Syria Age: 55 ⏳ Since 1993–present 🎭 Comedy, Historical
Who is Bassem Yakhour? Bassem Yakhour is a Syrian actor and comedian known for his sharp satire, character work, and strong theatre background. He became widely recognised through the sketch comedy series Spot Light, where he showcased impersonations and social humour, and has since built a career spanning drama, comedy, and television hosting across the Arab world.
