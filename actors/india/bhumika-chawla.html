Bhumika Chawla

India Actor, Actress

🌍 India 🎂 1978-08-22 📍 New Delhi, India Age: 48 ⏳ Since 1978 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Bhumika Chawla? Bhumika Chawla (born Rachna Chawla on 21 August 1978) is an Indian actress. She made her film debut in the Telugu film Yuvakudu (2000) and has since then worked in various Indian film industries and appeared in over thirty films in Telugu, Tamil, Hindi, Bhojpuri, Punjabi and Malayalam. She is probably best known for her critically acclaimed performances in Kushi, Okkadu (2003), Tere Naam, Missamma, Gandhi, My Fatherand Anasuya.
