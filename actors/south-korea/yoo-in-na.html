About Yoo In Na Yoo Inna (born June 5, 1982) is a South Korean actress. She made her acting debut as a supporting actress with High Kick Through the Roof (2009) before receiving her first leading role in the 2012 time-slip romance drama Queen Inhyun's Man. She is most known for starring in My Love From the Star (2013), Goblin (2016), Touch Your Heart (2019), and Snowdrop (2021).
