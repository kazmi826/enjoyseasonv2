Who is Keiko Toda? Keiko Toda (戸田 恵子, Toda Keiko, born September 12, 1957) is a Japanese actress, voice actress (seiyuu), narrator, singer and talent born in Nagoya City, Aichi Prefecture. She was also active as an enka singer under the name Yumi Ayu (あゆ 朱美, Ayu Yumi).
