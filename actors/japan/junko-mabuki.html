Junko Mabuki

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1955-02-22 📍 Beppu, Ōita Prefecture, Japan Age: 71 ⏳ Since 1955 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Junko Mabuki? Former actress (1978-1981) and the second "SM Queen" of Nikkatsu. Mabuki started working in gravure modeling before acting in small roles for Toei. After placing third in the Nikkatsu New Actress Contest, she acted for Roman Porno and in 1980, became the "SM Queen" after Naomi Tani's retirement. During this time, she also released music.
