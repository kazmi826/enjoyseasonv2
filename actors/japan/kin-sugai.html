Kin Sugai

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1926-02-28 📍 Tokyo, Japan Age: 100 ⏳ Since 1926 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Kin Sugai? Kin Sugai (菅井きん Sugai Kin) (28 February 1926 – 10 August 2018) was a Japanese actress. She won the award for best supporting actress at the 9th Hochi Film Award for The Funeral. Sugai is famous for her role in the jidaigeki drama Hissatsu series.
