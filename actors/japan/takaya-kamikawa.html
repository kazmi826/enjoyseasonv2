Who is Takaya Kamikawa? Takaya Kamikawa (上川 隆也, Kamikawa Takaya , May 7, 1965) is a Japanese actor, voice actor and talent from Hachioji, Tokyo. He belongs to Zero Light Years. Previously, he was affiliated with Caramel Box and Me & Her Corporation. He is 175 cm and has blood type A.
