About Natsuki Hanae Natsuki Hanae is a Japanese voice actor from Kanagawa Prefecture. He is affiliated with Across Entertainment. In 2015, he won an award at the 9th Seiyuu Awards as one of the best male newcomers. He also hosts the radio show ŌHana along with voice actor Ryōta Ōsaka. "Hanae" is actually a pseudonym; his real surname has not been disclosed by his agency.
