Ayumi Kinoshita

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1982-12-13 📍 Chita, Aichi, Japan Age: 44 ⏳ Since 1982 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Ayumi Kinoshita? Ayumi Kinoshita is a Japanese actress and voice actress. She was affiliated with Wave Management Nagoya, Parts, and Stardust Promotion before working as a freelance actress. She is well known for playing Marika Reimon/Deka Yellow in the 2004 television series Tokusou Sentai Dekaranger.
