Reina Ueda

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1994-01-17 📍 Toyama, Japan Age: 32 ⏳ Since 1994 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Reina Ueda? Reina Ueda is a Japanese voice actress and singer from Toyama Prefecture. She is affiliated with 81 Produce. Beginning her career as a voice actress in 2012, her first main role was Naru Sekiya, the protagonist of the 2014 anime series Hanayamata.
