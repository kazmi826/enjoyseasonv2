Asami Usuda

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1984-10-17 📍 Matsudo, Chiba Prefecture, Japan Age: 42 ⏳ Since 1984 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Asami Usuda? Asami Usuda (臼田 あさ美, Usuda Asami, real name: 臼田 麻美 (before marriage, same reading), October 17, 1984) is a Japanese actress and model. She is affiliated with A-Team. She was spotted and scouted outside the famous Shibuya 109 fashion compartment store in Tokyo at age 15 and began appearing in magazines as a model. Her husband is Reiji Okamoto, drummer of the rock band Okamoto's. She currently lives in Tokyo.
