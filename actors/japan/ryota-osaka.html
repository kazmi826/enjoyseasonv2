Ryota Osaka

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1986-08-02 📍 Tokushima, Japan Age: 40 ⏳ Since 1986 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Ryota Osaka? Ryota Osaka (逢坂良太) is a Japanese voice actor from Tokushima Prefecture who is affiliated with Early Wing. He received Best Male Newcomer at 9th Seiyu Awards in 2015. He also hosts the radio show ŌHana (逢坂市立花江学園 Ōsaka Shiritsu Hanae Gakuen) along with voice actor Natsuki Hanae.
