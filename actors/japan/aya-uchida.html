Aya Uchida

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1986-07-23 📍 Ota, Gunma, Japan Age: 40 ⏳ Since 1986 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Aya Uchida? Aya Uchida is a Japanese voice actress and singer. She played Kotori Minami in Love Live! School Idol Project and Kaban in Kemono Friends. She released the single "Sign/Candy Flavor", the song "Sign" was used as the ending theme for The Quintessential Quintuplets. Her nickname is Ucchi.
