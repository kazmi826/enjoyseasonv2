Who is Satomi Arai? Satomi Arai (新井 里美, Arai Satomi, July 4, 1980) is a Japanese voice actor from Yono, Saitama. She is the representative director of the voice actor management company Arai Voice Works. She is married to fellow voice talent Yoshimitsu Shimoyama.
