Who is Toshihiko Seki? Toshihiko Seki, born on June 11, 1962, in Sendai, Miyagi Prefecture, Japan, is a celebrated Japanese voice actor, actor, singer, and narrator. He has been active in the entertainment industry since 1983 and is affiliated with 81 Produce, a prominent talent agency. Seki is known for his versatility, often voicing characters with serious or easily agitated personalities, such as Duo Maxwell in Mobile Suit Gundam Wing, Iruka Umino in Naruto, Kaien Shiba in Bleach and Riki in Ai no Kusabi. He has also portrayed deranged villains like Legato Bluesummers in Trigun and Muzan Kibutsuji in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.
