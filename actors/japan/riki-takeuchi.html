🌍 Japan 🎂 1964-01-04 📍 Saiki, Ōita Prefecture, Japan Age: 62 ⏳ Since 1964 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Riki Takeuchi? Riki Takeuchi is a Japanese actor best known for his roles in v-cinema yakuza movies. Takeuchi has starred in many yakuza films and action films, such as Dead or Alive by Takashi Miike. Takeuchi also starred in Battle Royale II: Requiem, in which he played a fictionalized version of himself.
