🌍 Japan 🎂 1980-02-17 📍 Yamagata, Japan 🎂 Age: 46 🎬 46 Movies 📺 105 Dramas 🎭 Drama🎭 Action🎭 Romance
About Aya Endo Aya Endo is a Japanese voice actress and narrator from Yamagata Prefecture. At the 2nd Seiyu Awards, she won Best Singing along with her cast mates in Lucky Star, and at the 3rd Seiyu Awards, she won Best Supporting Actress. She is the current official Japanese dub voice of Minnie Mouse since 2016 replacing the late Yūko Mizutani.
