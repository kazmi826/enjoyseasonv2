🌍 Japan 🎂 1947-01-08 📍 Kyoto, Japan 🎂 Age: 79 🎬 159 Movies 📺 44 Dramas 🎭 Drama🎭 Action🎭 Romance
About Ittoku Kishibe Ittoku Kishibe (岸部一徳) is a Japanese actor, bassist, and lyricist. He originally entered showbusiness as the bassist for the Japanese rock bands The Tigers, PYG, and Inoue Takayuki Band, but later switched to acting. The veteran of over 115 films, he won the Japanese Academy Award for Outstanding Actor in a Leading Role in 1991 for "The Sting of Death," and was nominated for the Best Supporting Actor award in 1994. He has an agency, UN et NEUF, and he is the president of Kenji Sawada's private office "Kokoro Co., Ltd."
