🌍 Japan 🎂 1989-08-15 📍 Tokyo, Japan Age: 37 ⏳ Since 1989 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Masaki Okada? Masaki Okada (岡田 将生 Okada Masaki, born August 15, 1989 in Tokyo) is a Japanese actor. He is best known for his roles as Sekime Kyogo in the drama Hanazakari no Kimitachi e and as Takuma Kakinouchi in the 2009 film I Give My First Love to You.
