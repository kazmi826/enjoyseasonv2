Fumiko Orikasa

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1974-12-27 📍 Taito, Tokyo, Japan Age: 52 ⏳ Since 1974 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Fumiko Orikasa? Fumiko Orikasa ( born December 27, 1974 in Taito, Tokyo) is a Japanese voice actress and singer who works for Atomic Monkey. Her record label is Geneon Entertainment. Shopping is one of her hobbies. She is a skilled dancer, and is referred to as "Oririn" by fellow voice actors and fans. She also performed a fair number of anime insert and character image songs.
