Hiyori Sakurada

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 2002-12-19 📍 Chiba, Japan Age: 24 ⏳ Since 2002 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Hiyori Sakurada? Sakurada Hiyori is a Japanese child actress and model. She belongs to Ken-on entertainment agency. In 2014, she appeared as one of the children living in an orphanage in the drama "Tomorrow, there is no mom", which is a successful work. In 2018, she was selected as 6 out of a total of 3,437 applicants in the exclusive model audition of the teen magazine "Seventeen" ( Shueisha ) and she was selected as the "Miss Seventeen 2018".
