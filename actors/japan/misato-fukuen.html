Who is Misato Fukuen? Misato Fukuen (福圓 美里, Fukuen Misato) is a Japanese actress and voice actress affiliated with StarCrew. Her real name is Misato Sakuragi (桜木 美里, Sakuragi Misato). She has also performed activities as a singer under the name of MIRI. She was previously affiliated with Sigma Seven.
