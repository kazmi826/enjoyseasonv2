Who is Nobuhiko Okamoto? Nobuhiko Okamoto is a Japanese voice actor and singer from Tokyo, Japan, affiliated with Raccoon Dog. Previously, he was affiliated with the Pro-Fit agency. He won the Best New Actor Award on Third Seiyu Awards and Best Supporting Actor Award on Fifth Seiyu Awards.
