Rentaro Mikuni

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1923-01-20 📍 Ota, Gunma, Japan Age: 103 ⏳ Since 1923 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Rentaro Mikuni? Rentaro Mikuni (三國 連太郎, Mikuni Rentarō, born January 20, 1923) was a Japanese actor from Gunma Prefecture. He appeared in over 150 films since making his screen debut in 1951, and won three Japanese Academy Awards for Best Actor, and a further seven nominations. He also won two Blue Ribbon Awards for Best Actor, in 1960 and in 1989. Kōichi Satō is his son.
