Who is Takaya Hashi? Takaya Hashi (土師 孝也 Hashi Takaya, September 8, 1952 – August 27, 2025) was a veteran Japanese voice actor and actor affiliated with Apte Pro. His real name was Takaya Katō (加藤 孝也 Katō Takaya).
