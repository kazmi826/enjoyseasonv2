Who is Shun Horie? Shun Horie (堀江 瞬, Horie Shun, May 25, 1993) is a Japanese voice actor born in Osaka. He won the Best New Actor Award at the 12th Seiyu Awards. He is affiliated with Raccoon Dog and member of SparQlew. Previously, he was affiliated with Pro-Fit until March 2022.
