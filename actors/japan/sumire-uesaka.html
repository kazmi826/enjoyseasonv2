Who is Sumire Uesaka? Sumire Uesaka (上坂 すみれ, Uesaka Sumire, born December 19, 1991) is a Japanese voice actress, actress, talent, and singer from Kanagawa Prefecture. Her agency is Voice Kit and her record label is King Amusement Creative.
