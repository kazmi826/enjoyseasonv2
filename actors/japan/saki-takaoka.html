🌍 Japan 🎂 1972-12-03 📍 Kanagawa, Japan Age: 54 ⏳ Since 1972 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Saki Takaoka? Saki Takaoka (高岡 早紀) is a Japanese actress born as Sakiko Takaoka (高岡 佐紀子). Her uncle is the jazz pianist Yōsuke Yamashita. She won the award for best actress at the 19th Hochi Film Award for Crest of Betrayal.
