🌍 Japan 🎂 1963-11-12 📍 Tokyo, Japan Age: 63 ⏳ Since 1963 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Susumu Terajima? Susumu Terajima (寺島進) is a Japanese actor. He has appeared in more than 200 films, 15 commercials, and numerous television dramas in a 20-year career. He is rarely the star of the films he participates in but is widely respected for taking his work very seriously despite how small his role may be.
