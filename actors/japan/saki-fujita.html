About Saki Fujita Saki Fujita (藤田 咲, Fujita Saki, born October 19, 1984) is a Japanese voice actress and narrator from Tokyo. She is represented by Arts Vision. Her real name and former stage name is Sakiko Fujita (藤田 咲子, Fujita Sakiko). She sang the ending theme to the anime Tokimeki Memorial Only Love, "Kiseki no Kakera", (奇跡のかけら) along with Yuki Makishima and Yukako Yoshikawa as well as the opening songs for Working!! - Someone Else, Coolish Walk, Now!!!Gamble - with Kana Asumi and Eri Kitamura. She is best known for voicing Ymir from Attack on Titan, as well as for sampling her voice for Crypton Future Media's Vocaloid, Hatsune Miku.
