Katsuo Nakamura

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1938-04-23 📍 Akasaka, Minato, Tokyo, Japan Age: 88 ⏳ Since 1938 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Katsuo Nakamura? Katsuo Nakamura is a Japanese actor. He won the award for best supporting actor at the 5th Japan Academy Prize and at the 6th Hochi Film Award for Kagero-za, Buriki no kunsho, Shikake-nin Baian. He is the younger brother of the actor Kinnosuke Yorozuya. Description above from the Wikipedia article Katsuo Nakamura, licensed under CC-BY-SA, full list of contributors on Wikipedia.
