Maki Sakai

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1970-05-17 📍 Taito, Tokyo Prefecture, Japan Age: 56 ⏳ Since 1970 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Maki Sakai? Maki Sakai (坂井 真紀) (born 17 May 1970 in Taito, Tokyo) is a Japanese actress. The fourth rehouse girl of Mitsui, she has starred in a number of TV drama series such as Daburu kitchin (1993) and Watashi no unmei (1994). She is a graduate of Jumonji Junior College.
