Aoi Yuuki

Japan Actor, Actress

🌍 Japan 🎂 1992-03-27 📍 Chiba, Japan Age: 34 ⏳ Since 1992 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Aoi Yuuki? Aoi Yuki (悠木 碧, Yūki Aoi, born March 27, 1992) is a Japanese voice actress (seiyuu), actress and singer from Sanmu City, Chiba Prefecture. She is affiliated with Aoni Production. Her real name and former stage name is Aoi Yabusaki (八武 崎碧, Yabusaki Aoi). She was previously affiliated with Breath and Pro Fit.
