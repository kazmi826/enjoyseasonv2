Who is Sota Fukushi? Sota Fukushi (福士 蒼汰 Fukushi Sōta, born May 30, 1993) is a Japanese actor. He rose to prominence portraying Gentaro Kisaragi in the Kamen Rider franchise, and has since starred in television series Koinaka (2016) and My Lover's Secret (2017), as well as films Strobe Edge (2015), My Tomorrow, Your Yesterday (2016) and Bleach (2018)
