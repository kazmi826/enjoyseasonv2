Who is Yoji Ueda? Yoji Ueda (上田 燿司, Ueda Yōji) is a Japanese voice actor formerly affiliated with Mausu Promotion and currently affiliated with Amuleto. His name was Yoji Ueda (上田 陽司, Ueda Yōji) until April 1, 2008.
