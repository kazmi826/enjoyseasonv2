Claire Keim

France Actor, Actress

🌍 France 🎂 1975-07-08 📍 Senlis, Oise, France Age: 51 ⏳ Since 1975 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Claire Keim? Claire Keim (born Claire Lefebvre 8 July 1975) is a French actress and singer. Keim was born in Senlis, Oise to an architect father and a dentist mother. She is in a relationship with French footballer Bixente Lizarazu, and lives in Saint Jean de Luz and Paris. She gave birth to their daughter Uhaina in August 2008.
