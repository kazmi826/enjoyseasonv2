Alba Rohrwacher

Italian Actress

🌍 Italy 🎂 1979-02-23 📍 Florence, Italy Age: 47 ⏳ Since 2006 🎭 Drama, Art, Social
Who is Alba Rohrwacher? Alba Rohrwacher is an Italian actress. In 2009, she received a David di Donatello for Best Actress for her performance in Pupi Avati's Giovanna's Father. On 6 September 2014, Rohrwacher won the Volpi Cup for Best Actress at the 71st Venice International Film Festival for the film Hungry Hearts.
