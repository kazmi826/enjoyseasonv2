About Micaela Ramazzotti Micaela Ramazzotti (born 17 January 1979) is an Italian actress. Her film credits include Non prendere impegni stasera, The First Beautiful Thing and Il cuore grande delle ragazze. Ramazzotti was a regular cast member of the show Crimini bianchi.She won the David di Donatello for Best Actress for her role in The First Beautiful Thing in 2010. She married film director, writer and producer Paolo Virzì, on January 17, 2009, in Livorno. Together they have 2 children: Jacopo (born 1 March 2010) and Anna (15 April 2013).
