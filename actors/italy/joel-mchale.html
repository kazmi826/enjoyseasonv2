Joel McHale

Italy Actor, Actress

🌍 Italy 🎂 1971-11-20 📍 Roma, Italy Age: 55 ⏳ Since 1971 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Joel McHale? Joel Edward McHale (born November 20, 1971) is an American stand-up comedian, actor, writer, television producer, television personality, and voice artist. He is best known for hosting The Soup and for his role as Jeff Winger on Community. He is of Irish-American descent and European-Canadian descent. He voiced Elliot in Open Season 2, filling in for Ashton Kutcher. He also appeared in Spider-Man 2 in a cameo appearance as bank manager Mr. Jacks, CSI: Miami as Greg Welsh, and Spy Kids 4: All the Time in the World as Wilbur.
