About Bruna Marquezine Bruna Reis Maia (born August 4, 1995), better known as Bruna Marquezine, is a Brazilian actress. Marquezine began her television career in 2000. Since then she has acted in many Brazilian shows and films. She played her first role in an English-speaking film when she played Roseli in Breaking Through (2015). However, her biggest role in Hollywood has been her role in Blue Beetle (2023), when she played Jenny Kord, the love interest of Jaime Reyes.
