About Davika Hoorne Davika Hoorne (Thai: ดาวิกา โฮร์เน่), also known as Mai Davika, is a Thai actress and model, who made an acting debut on 2010 in television series Ngao Kammathep as the lead role. She rose to fame from her films; Heart Attack with Sunny Suwanmethanon and Suddenly Twenty. She is the female lead star of Thailand's highest-grossing film of all time, Pee Mak alongside Mario Maurer.
