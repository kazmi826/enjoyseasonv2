Apu Biswas

Actor, Actress

🌍 🎂 1989-10-08 📍 Age: 37 ⏳ Since 1989 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Apu Biswas? Apu Biswas is a Bangladeshi film actress, model and the former wife of notable Bangladeshi actor Shakib Khan. Biswas made her debut in the film industry with the film Kal Shokale in 2006. She then performed in Koti Takar Kabin opposite Shakib Khan for the first time. Shakib Khan secretly married actress Apu Biswas in 2008. They worked together in 72 movies. In 2022 her films include Ajker Shortcut.
