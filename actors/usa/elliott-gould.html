Elliott Gould

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1938-08-29 📍 Brooklyn, New York City, New York, USA Age: 88 ⏳ Since 1938 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Elliott Gould? Elliott Gould (born August 29, 1938) is an American actor. He began acting in Hollywood films during the 1960s, and has remained prolific ever since. Some of his most notable films include M*A*S*H and Bob & Carol & Ted & Alice, for which he received an Oscar nomination. In recent years, he has starred as Reuben Tishkoff in Ocean's Eleven, Ocean's Twelve, and Ocean's Thirteen.
