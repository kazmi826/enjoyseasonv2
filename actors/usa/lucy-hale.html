Lucy Hale

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1989-06-14 📍 Memphis, Tennessee, USA Age: 37 ⏳ Since 1989 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Lucy Hale? Karen Lucille Hale (born June 14, 1989), better known as Lucy Hale, is an American actress and singer. She is known for her roles as Becca Sommers in Bionic Woman, Rose Baker in Privileged, Sherrie in the horror film Scream 4, and as Aria Montgomery on the hit ABC Family series Pretty Little Liars. She is also known for her appearance on the reality show American Juniors.
