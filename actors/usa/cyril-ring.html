Cyril Ring

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1892-12-05 📍 Boston, Massachusetts, USA Age: 134 ⏳ Since 1892 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Cyril Ring? From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Cyril Ring (December 5, 1892 – July 17, 1967) was an American film actor. He began his career in silent films in 1921. By the time of his final performance in 1951, he had appeared in over 350 films, almost all in small and/or uncredited parts. He is probably best remembered today for his role as Harvey Yates, a con artist captured and hand-cuffed to fellow con artist Penelope, played by Kay Francis at the very end of the Marx Brothers first film The Cocoanuts (1929).
