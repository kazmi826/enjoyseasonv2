Ralph Macchio

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1961-11-04 📍 Huntington, Long Island, New York, USA Age: 65 ⏳ Since 1961 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Ralph Macchio? Ralph George Macchio (born November 4, 1961) is an American businessman, entrepreneur, actor, producer, director and author. He is best known for his roles as Daniel LaRusso in the Karate Kid series, Billy Gambini in My Cousin Vinny, and Johnny Cade in The Outsiders. He is also known to American television audiences for his season five recurring role as Jeremy Andretti on the comedy-drama Eight Is Enough. He competed on the twelfth season of Dancing with the Stars.
