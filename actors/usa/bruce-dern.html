Bruce Dern

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1936-06-04 📍 Chicago, Illinois, USA Age: 90 ⏳ Since 1936 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Bruce Dern? Bruce MacLeish Dern (born June 4, 1936) is an American actor. He was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor for Coming Home (1978) and the Academy Award for Best Actor for Nebraska (2013). His other major film appearances include Silent Running (1972), The King of Marvin Gardens (1972), The Cowboys (1972), Posse (1975), Family Plot (1976), Black Sunday (1977), Tattoo (1981), Monster (2003), The Hateful Eight (2015), and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood (2019).
