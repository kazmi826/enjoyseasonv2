John Heard

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1946-03-07 📍 Washington, District of Columbia, USA Age: 80 ⏳ Since 1946 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is John Heard? John Heard Jr. (March 7, 1946 – July 21, 2017) was an American actor. Heard made his debut appearance in film with the ensemble Between the Lines (1977). He appeared in a number of successful films, including Heart Beat (1980), Cutter's Way (1981), Cat People (1982), Beaches (1988), and Deceived (1991), and was perhaps best known for his role of Peter McCallister in the first two Home Alone films (1990–1992). He was nominated for an Emmy Award in 1999 for guest-starring as Vin Makazian on The Sopranos (1999–2004).
