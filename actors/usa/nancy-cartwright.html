🌍 USA 🎂 1957-10-25 📍 Dayton, Ohio, USA Age: 69 ⏳ Since 1957 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Nancy Cartwright? Nancy Campbell Cartwright (born October 25, 1957) is an American film and television actress, comedian and voice artist. She is best known for her long-running role as Bart Simpson on The Simpsons. She is also the aunt of pop star Sabrina Carpenter.
