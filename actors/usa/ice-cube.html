Ice Cube

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1969-06-15 📍 Los Angeles, California, USA Age: 57 ⏳ Since 1969 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Ice Cube? O'Shea Jackson Sr. (born June 15, 1969), better known by his stage name Ice Cube, is an American rapper, record producer, and filmmaker. He began his career as a member of the hip-hop group C.I.A. and later joined N.W.A. He is known for his roles in the films “Boyz n the Hood,” “Friday,” the notorious remake of "War of the Worlds" and the “Jump Street” series.
