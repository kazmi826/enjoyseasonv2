Dee Wallace

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1948-12-14 📍 Kansas City, Kansas, USA Age: 78 ⏳ Since 1948 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Dee Wallace? Dee Wallace (born 14 December 1948) is an American actress and comedienne. She is perhaps best known for her roles in several popular films. These include the starring role as Elliot's mother in the Steven Spielberg film E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial (1982), her most widely seen role. She also played key roles in popular cult films The Hills Have Eyes (1977) and The Howling (1981) and appeared in The Stepford Wives in 1975 and 10 (1979). In total, Wallace has appeared in more than 85 films.
