Jack Quaid

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1992-04-24 📍 Los Angeles, California, USA Age: 34 ⏳ Since 1992 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Jack Quaid? Jack Henry Quaid (born April 24, 1992) is an American actor. He debuted his acting with a minor role in the dystopian film The Hunger Games (2012). His breakout role was as vigilante Hughie Campbell in the satirical superhero series The Boys (2019–present). Quaid was part of the main cast of the drama series Vinyl (2016). He has voiced Brad Boimler in the series Star Trek: Lower Decks (2020–2024) and Superman in My Adventures with Superman (2023–present). In film, he has appeared in Logan Lucky (2017), starred as Richie Kirsch in the slasher film Scream (2022), and played theoretical physicist Richard Feynman in Oppenheimer (2023).
