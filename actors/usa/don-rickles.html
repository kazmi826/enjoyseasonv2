Don Rickles

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1926-05-08 📍 Queens, New York City, New York, USA Age: 100 ⏳ Since 1926 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Don Rickles? Donald Jay Rickles (May 8, 1926 – April 6, 2017) was an American stand-up comedian and actor, known primarily for his insult comedy. His film roles include Run Silent, Run Deep (1958), Enter Laughing (1967), Kelly's Heroes (1970), and Casino (1995). From 1976 to 1978, Rickles had a two-season starring role in the NBC television sitcom C.P.O. Sharkey, having previously starred in two eponymous half-hour programs, an ABC variety show titled The Don Rickles Show (1968) and a CBS sitcom identically titled The Don Rickles Show (1972).
