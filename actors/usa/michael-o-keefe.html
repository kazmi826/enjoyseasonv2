Michael O'Keefe

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1955-04-24 📍 Mount Vernon, New York, USA Age: 71 ⏳ Since 1955 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Michael O'Keefe? Michael Raymond O'Keefe (born April 24, 1955) is an American film and television actor. He is best known for his roles as Danny Noonan in Caddyshack, Ben Meechum in The Great Santini (for which he received a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor), and Darryl Palmer in The Slugger's Wife. On television, he had a recurring roles as Fred in Roseanne from 1993 to 1995 and more recently in the series Homeland and Sneaky Pete.
