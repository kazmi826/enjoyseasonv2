Jane Krakowski

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1968-10-11 📍 Parsippany, New Jersey, USA Age: 58 ⏳ Since 1968 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Jane Krakowski? Jane Krakowski (born October 11, 1968) is an American actress and singer. She is known for playing Elaine Vassal on Ally McBeal and Jenna Maroney on 30 Rock, winning Screen Actors Guild Awards for both roles. She also regularly performs on the stage, winning a Tony Award for her performance in Nine, and an Olivier Award for Guys and Dolls in London's West End.
