Adrienne Palmer

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1988-08-31 📍 Garland, Texas, USA Age: 38 ⏳ Since 1988 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Adrienne Palmer? Adrienne Palmer (née Reese; born August 31, 1988), better known by her ring name Athena, is an American professional wrestler currently signed to All Elite Wrestling (AEW). She primarily appears in AEW's sister promotion Ring of Honor (ROH), where she is the leader of the M.I.T stable and the current ROH Women's World Champion in her first reign; at over 1,100 days, she is the longest-reigning champion in ROH history. She is also known for her time in WWE under the name Ember Moon, where she became a former one-time NXT Women's Champion and one-time NXT Women's Tag Team Champion. Before going to WWE, she wrestled on the independent circuit as Athena for promotions such as Shimmer Women Athletes and Women Superstars Uncensored, and still makes sporadic independent appearances while with AEW
