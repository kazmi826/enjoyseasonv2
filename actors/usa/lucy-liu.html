🌍 USA 🎂 1968-12-02 📍 New York City, New York, USA Age: 58 ⏳ Since 1968 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Lucy Liu? Lucy Alexis Liu (born December 2, 1968) is an American actress, producer, and artist. Widely regarded as a trailblazer for Asian American representation in Hollywood, she is the recipient of numerous accolades, including two Critics' Choice Awards, two Screen Actors Guild Awards, and nominations for two Primetime Emmy Awards.
