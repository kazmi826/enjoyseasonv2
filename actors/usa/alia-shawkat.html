Alia Shawkat

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1989-04-18 📍 Riverside, California, USA Age: 37 ⏳ Since 1989 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Alia Shawkat? Alia Martine Shawkat (born April 18, 1989) is an American actress. She is known for her performances as Maeby Fünke in the Fox/Netflix television sitcom Arrested Development (2003–2006; 2013–2019), Dory Sief in the TBS and HBO Max dark comedy series Search Party (2016–2022), and Gertie Michaels in the 2015 horror-comedy film The Final Girls, as well as her roles in State of Grace and The Old Man. She has also guest-starred as historical figures Frances Cleveland, Virginia Hall, and Alexander Hamilton on Comedy Central's Drunk History.
