Who is Clint Howard? Clint Howard (born April 20, 1959) is an American actor. A character actor with numerous brief appearances on television and films, he is usually noted for his unusual appearance and has had many bit parts in movies directed by his brother, Ron Howard. He is also the uncle of actress Bryce Dallas Howard.
