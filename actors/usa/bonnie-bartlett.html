Who is Bonnie Bartlett? Bonnie Bartlett Daniels (born June 20, 1929) is an American actress. She is known for her role as Grace Snider Edwards on Little House on the Prairie and as Ellen Craig on St. Elsewhere. She has been married to her St. Elsewhere co-star William Daniels since 1951; they have three children.
