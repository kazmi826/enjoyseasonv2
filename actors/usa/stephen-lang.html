Stephen Lang

USA Actor, Actress

🌍 USA 🎂 1952-07-11 📍 New York City, New York, USA Age: 74 ⏳ Since 1952 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Stephen Lang? Stephen Lang (born July 11, 1952) is an American actor and playwright. He started in theatre on Broadway but is well known for his film portrayals of Stonewall Jackson in Gods and Generals (2003), George Pickett in Gettysburg (1993), and his 2009 roles as Colonel Miles Quaritch in Avatar and as Texan lawman Charles Winstead in Public Enemies. Lang was the co-artistic director (along with Carlin Glynn and Lee Grant) of the famed Actor's Studio at its headquarters in New York City from 2004 to 2006.
