About Sung Kang Sung-Ho Kang (Korean: 강성호; RR: Gang Seongho; born April 8, 1972) is an American actor. His first significant role was as Han Lue in the Fast & Furious franchise, a character he first portrayed in Better Luck Tomorrow (2002). Kang also played John Mak in the television series Power.
