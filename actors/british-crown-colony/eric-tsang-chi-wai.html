Who is Eric Tsang Chi-Wai? Eric Tsang Chi-Wai (Chinese: 曾志偉; born 14 April 1953 in Hong Kong with family roots in Xinhui, Guangdong, China) is a prolific Hong Kong actor, film director, film producer and television host best known for hosting the Super Trio series on TVB over the course of 10 years.
