Susan Shaw Yam-Yam

British Crown Colony Actor, Actress

🌍 British Crown Colony 🎂 1950-08-17 📍 Hong Kong, British Crown Colony Age: 76 ⏳ Since 1950 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Susan Shaw Yam-Yam? Born in Hong Kong, Susan moved to Taiwan with her father at an early age, studying nursing for five years after graduation, and then working as a nurse on a ship. After travelling through 23 countries, she decided to go ashore in Hong Kong to find another job, first singing in a nightclub and then changing her name to Xiaoyin. She has been active in the Hong Kong film industry since 1970. She has won four Hong Kong Film Awards for Best Actress (2008, 2011, 2013, 2018).
