Martin Short

Canadian-American Actor, Comedian

🌍 Canada 🎂 March 26, 1950 📍 Hamilton, Canada Age: 76 ⏳ Since 1972-present 🎭 Comedy
Who is Martin Short? Martin Hayter Short, CM (born March 26, 1950) is a Canadian comedian, actor, writer, singer and producer. He is best known for his comedy work, particularly on the TV programs SCTV and Saturday Night Live. He has also starred in many popular comedic films such as Three Amigos, Innerspace, Pure Luck, Jungle 2 Jungle, Mars Attacks!, Father of the Bride, and Father of the Bride Part 2.
