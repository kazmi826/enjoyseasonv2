🌍 Canada 🎂 1975-05-09 📍 Toronto, Ontario, Canada Age: 51 ⏳ Since 1975 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Chris Diamantopoulos? Chris Diamantopoulos (born May 9, 1975) is a Canadian actor. He grew up splitting his time between Greece and Canada and at age 9 started doing TV commercials and professional theater. He left home at 18 to perform in a series of US national tours after which, he landed on Broadway. Diamantopoulos is best known for his supporting role in season nine of The Office and portraying Moe in the Farrelly Brothers' film The Three Stooges.
