About Saba Mubarak Saba Mubarak is a Jordanian actress who was born in 1976 in the village of Ajlun in Jordan. She received a bachelor's degree in theater acting and directing from Yarmouk University in 2001. She started her career in the series “Sihr wa Qamar” (Magic and a Moon) in 1998, after which she scored several successful roles in Jordanian and Syrian television like “Omar al Khayam,” “Belqeis” and “Al Arwah Al Mohajira” (The Immigrating Spirits). Saba's debut in Jordanian cinema was in 2003 through the film “Sifr Al Ganha.” She also appeared in the films “Khareg Al Taghtiya” (Out of Coverage) and “Mamlakat Al Naml” (Kingdom of the Ants).
