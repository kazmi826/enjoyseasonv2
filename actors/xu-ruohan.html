Who is Xu Ruohan? Xu Ruohan (徐若晗) Born on February 27, 1998 in Xi’an, Shaanxi Province, China, Xu Ruohan is a rising actress known for her versatile roles in romance, drama, and action-packed films and series. She made her mark with her role as Fareeda in the action blockbuster Vanguard (2020), starring alongside Jackie Chan.
