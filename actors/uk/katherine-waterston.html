Katherine Waterston

UK Actor, Actress

🌍 UK 🎂 1980-03-03 📍 London, England, UK Age: 46 ⏳ Since 1980 🎭 Drama, Action, Romance
Who is Katherine Waterston? Katherine Boyer Waterston is a British-American actress who has starred in films like Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (2016) and Steve Jobs (2015). The daughter of actor Sam Waterston and model Lynn Louisa Woodruff, she studied acting at NYU's Tisch School of the Arts. Waterston made her film debut in 2007 with roles in Michael Clayton and The Babysitters.
