Francesca Annis

UK Actor, Actress · Active since 1945

🌍 UK 🎂 1945-05-14 📍 Kensington, London, England, UK 🎂 Age: 81 🎬 55 Movies 📺 34 Dramas 🎭 Drama🎭 Action🎭 Romance
About Francesca Annis Francesca Annis (born 14 May 1945) is an English actress. She is known for television roles in Reckless (1998), Wives and Daughters (1999), Deceit (2000) and Cranford (2007). A six-time BAFTA TV Award nominee, she won the 1979 BAFTA TV Award for Best Actress for the ITV serial Lillie. Her film appearances include Krull (1983), Dune (1984), The Debt Collector (1999) and The Libertine (2004).
