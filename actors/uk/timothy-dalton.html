Timothy Dalton

UK Actor, Actress · Active since 1946

🌍 UK 🎂 1946-03-21 📍 Colwyn Bay, Wales, UK 🎂 Age: 80 🎬 57 Movies 📺 28 Dramas 🎭 Drama🎭 Action🎭 Romance
About Timothy Dalton Timothy Leonard Dalton Leggett (born March 21, 1946) is a British actor. He gained international prominence as the fourth actor to portray fictional secret agent James Bond in the Eon Productions film series, starring in The Living Daylights (1987) and Licence to Kill (1989). Beginning his career on stage, he made his film debut as Philip II of France in the 1968 historical drama The Lion in Winter. He took roles in the period films Wuthering Heights (1970), Cromwell (1970), and Mary, Queen of Scots (1971). Dalton also appeared in the films Flash Gordon (1980), The Rocketeer (1991), Looney Tunes: Back in Action (2003), Hot Fuzz (2007) and The Tourist (2010).
