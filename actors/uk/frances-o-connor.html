Frances O'Connor

UK Actor, Actress · Active since 1967

🌍 UK 🎂 1967-06-12 📍 Wantage, Oxfordshire, England, UK 🎂 Age: 59 🎬 31 Movies 📺 16 Dramas 🎭 Drama🎭 Action🎭 Romance
About Frances O'Connor Frances Ann O'Connor (born 12 June 1967) is a British-Australian actress and director. She is known for her roles in the films Mansfield Park (1999), Bedazzled (2000), A.I. Artificial Intelligence (2001), The Importance of Being Earnest (2002), and Timeline (2003). O'Connor has won an AACTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role for her performance in Blessed (2009), and earned Golden Globe Award for Best Actress – Miniseries or Television Film nominations for her performances in Madame Bovary (2000) and The Missing (2014).
